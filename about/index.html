About Me

I’m Hao Chen. I’m currently a programmer at MISingulariti which is a startup in Beijing. It’s about Natural Language Understanding and App Search. I have always been fascinated by science and technology and especially machine learning.

This blog is to keep trace of my life and to share some technology and science knowledge. I research things I find interesting, then document it with a blog post to share my understanding. Hopefully it’s able to help people to find things helpful or interesting. I welcome email and comments about all these topics, more or less.

Before that, I was an undergraduate at Peking University majoring Computer Science. Also, I had been to UCLA as an exchange student at my fourth year of undergraduate. I’ve been studying algorithms for many years. I’ve been to ACM-ICPC World Final 2015.
